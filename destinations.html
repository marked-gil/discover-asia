Where We Can Bring You:

Philippines

Discover the gems of the Philippines by visiting their breath-taking wonders of nature such as the Chocolate Hills, the Rice Terraces, and the crystal clear water of Boracay among others.

Japan

Japan is a great place to visit as it is home to 21 World Heritage sites. It also boasts a very efficient transportation system, amazingly clean streets, and wonderfully polite and friendly people. And let’s not forget the delicious and sumptuous Japanese cuisine.

Thailand

Thailand is the land of thousands of Buddhist temples. It is also famous for its street foods, tropical islands with crystal clear waters, and affordable shopping. Also try to ride on their popular Tuk Tuk, which is a 3-wheeled vehicle, or experience their floating market.

China

China is very well known for its rich history and culture. But, they have so much more to offer. From its spectacular natural landscapes to its awe-inspiring man-made wonders, and to their ‘world staple’ Chinese cuisines, you will marvel at the wonder that is China.

Maldives

Want a luxury vacation at its finest? You will never go wrong when you spend your holiday in Maldives. With its majestic waters with rich marine life, and private resorts with first-class services, you will feel like living in paradise.

Singapore

If you are looking for a holiday with abundant selection of food and restaurants - from the fanciest to the simplest, Singapore could be your next destination. You could also enjoy the view at Marina Bay Sands, and marvel at the beauty of its Botanic Gardens, plus more.

Hong Kong

If shopping is in your mind for your trip, Hong Kong might be the best place for you. Here you have all the choices a shopper can dream of – from all the high-end brands to the most affordable Chinese authentic goods. Also, you get to see its amazing skylines brought about by its stunning skyscrapers.

Vietnam

Friendly people, popular and delicious Asian cuisines, unfrequented yet amazing beaches and bays, and value for the money – all these you can find in Vietnam.

Indonesia

Indonesia has a lot of things to boast about, but few of the finest are its myriads of islands and beaches, several of which are among the best in the world.

Malaysia

Malaysia is a country of cultural diversity being home for Malays, Chinese, and Indians. Also, it prides itself for its awesome beaches and islands. It also has one of the best diving destinations in the world.
